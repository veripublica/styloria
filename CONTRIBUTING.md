# Contributing

`styloria` is dual-licensed under AGPL-3.0-only OR a commercial license
(`LicenseRef-veripublica-Commercial`). Selling commercial licenses requires
the copyright holder to hold full copyright over the entire codebase.

**As a result: external contributions (PRs) are not being accepted yet.**
A Contributor License Agreement (or equivalent copyright assignment) must be
in place before any external code can be merged, and that mechanism has not
been finalized yet (shared across `veripublica` house-brand projects).

If you'd like to contribute in the future, feel free to open an issue to
discuss the idea — just don't expect PRs to be merged until the CLA process
is live.
